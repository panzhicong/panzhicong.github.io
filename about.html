My AI Journey: Crafting Confidence with AI

For over 15 years, I've believed data's promise is to create clarity and understanding. My journey with AI is about making that promise a reality.

I saw this challenge at a custom manufacturing studio — brilliant artisans were frustrated. Miscommunication over designs led to waste, costly rework, and unhappy customers. They had years of data, but it was trapped in siloed systems, unable to help.

The result was more than metrics — a 15% increase in conversions and a 5% rise in sales. It was the team's renewed confidence. Data and AI don't replace human ingenuity; they amplify it to build trust and finally keep the promise data always held.
