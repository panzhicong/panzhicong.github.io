FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Stackbrains, and how can it help my business?

Stackbrains is an advanced AI-native operating platform designed to empower SME manufacturers to improve efficiency by 10X. It allows our SME customers to make informed decisions based on accurate, reliable and real-time data insights.

How does Stackbrains ensure data security and trust?

Stackbrains uses custom quality checks, machine learning-based monitoring, and AI-driven solutions to proactively identify and resolve data inconsistencies, ensuring your data is always accurate and trustworthy.

Can Stackbrains integrate with my existing data infrastructure?

Yes, Stackbrains integrates seamlessly with your existing data stack — whether cloud-based warehouses, on-premises databases, or a hybrid approach — providing comprehensive observability across all your data sources.

How does Stackbrains handle data security during implementation?

All data connections are encrypted, access controls are properly configured, and only authorized users have access to sensitive data. We provide audit logs to track user activities and data changes throughout.

How long does it take to fully implement Stackbrains?

Initial setup can be completed within a few days, with full implementation — including custom configurations and user training — taking between 2 and 4 weeks, depending on your operations’ complexity.

What are the system requirements for deploying Stackbrains?

Stackbrains is flexible and can be deployed on cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, Azure) or on-premises servers. Requirements depend on your data scale, but the platform is built for efficiency with minimal infrastructure overhead.
